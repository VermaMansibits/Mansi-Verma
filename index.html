A Bit About Me

Hey there! Currently, I am the Vice President at Morgan Stanley, leading initiatives within the Prime Brokerage group under the Institutional Securities Group. Prior to this, I served as Lead Software Engineer at Ford Model E (EV Division), where I was instrumental in designing and implementing the foundational notification backend platforms for Ford’s electric vehicles.
Before my time at Ford, I spent nearly a decade at Microsoft, where I advanced to the role of Senior Software Engineer on the Bing team. There, I played a key role in developing features that enhanced user engagement and search relevance across multiple platforms. My work at Microsoft also involved significant contributions to data mining, information retrieval, and natural language processing.
My professional journey began during my academic years, where I gained practical experience through internships at Microsoft (Bing Team), HP Labs, Bangalore, Bhabha Atomic Research Centre, Mumbai, and the Defense Institute of Advanced Technology, Pune. During my undergraduate studies at the Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani - Goa Campus, I actively contributed as a Teaching Assistant for the Computer Programming-1 course and served as the chief coordinator for the departmental magazine, Building Bytes (2008). I went on to pursue a Master’s degree in Computer Science from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), where I further honed my skills.
My areas of expertise include Algorithms, Data Mining, Databases, Cloud Computing, and Machine Learning. For more details on my projects, please visit my Projects page or view my Curriculum Vitae.
